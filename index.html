WELCOME, THIS IS ME:

As a highly skilled CNC machinist with significant experience in the aerospace industry, I bring a wealth of expertise in the production of precision components and assemblies.
I have a keen eye for detail, strong problem-solving skills and a commitment to quality control that has made me an asset to every organization I have worked with. My ability to interpret complex engineering drawings and CAD models, as well as my proficiency in using MASTERCAM software, has helped me consistently meet or exceed production goals. Additionally, I possess excellent communication and interpersonal skills and I value working collaboratively as part of a team. I am confident that my experience and skills would enable me to effectively contribute to any organization's success.

About Me

I graduated from Lincoln Tech Institute in 2020 as a CNC Manufacturing Technician, where I honed my skills in precision machining and advanced manufacturing techniques. My journey in the field began in 2013 when I graduated from a technical high school as an Industrial Mechanic, gaining a solid foundation in mechanical systems and industrial processes. This combination of education and hands-on experience has equipped me with the expertise to excel in CNC machining and manufacturing.
